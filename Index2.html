Mental Health Awareness

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act.

Effects of Mental Health

Untreated mental health issues can lead to various problems such as anxiety, depression, and impaired daily functioning.

How to Reduce Mental Health Issues

Reducing mental health issues involves seeking support, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, and practicing self-care.
